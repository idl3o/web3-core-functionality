Create and secure your own blockchain in this interactive simulation. Learn how blocks are formed, transactions are validated, and consensus is reached.

  • Understand blockchain fundamentals through hands-on experience
  • Learn about consensus mechanisms and network security
  • Defend your blockchain against simulated attacks

Token Trader

Test your trading skills in a simulated DEX environment. Learn about liquidity pools, impermanent loss, and trading strategies.

Market Simulator
