Benefits of IPFS Integration

  • Decentralized Storage

    Your content is distributed across the IPFS network, not controlled by a single entity.

  • Censorship Resistance

    No central authority can remove your content from the network.

  • Content Addressing

    Your content has a unique CID that verifies its authenticity and prevents tampering.

  • Global Distribution

    Content is served from the nearest available node, improving delivery speed.

  • Permanent Availability

    As long as one node hosts your content, it remains available on the network.
